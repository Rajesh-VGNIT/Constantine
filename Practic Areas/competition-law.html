Competition Law

Home Competition Law
Constantine Advisory Services competition law team provides comprehensive, cutting-edge antitrust and competition law solutions to its clients. The team has been involved in some of the most high-profile antitrust matters in competition law in India and routinely represents complainants as well as defending companies (including leniency applicants) before the Supreme Court, National Company Law Appellate Tribunal, Competition Commission of India, Director General as well as various High Courts.

The team has in-depth knowledge and experience in dealing with merger control issues. The team’s strong experience in dealing with complex cross border transactions, acquisitions, mergers, and joint ventures, assist in appropriately advising the clients for devising merger control strategy for obtaining approval from the CCI.

The team has profound experience in specialized sectors such as e-commerce, technology, telecommunications, shipping, automobile, cement, retail and consumer goods and understands nuances of each sector which helps them to effectively protect clients’ unique interests.

The team also routinely advises on a range of competition law issues including:


  • Cartels and horizontal agreements

  • Vertical restraints (agreements)

  • Compliance (training) and audit

  • Dawn raid preparedness and training

  • Bidding (bid rigging) and public procurement

  • Follow-on damages

  • Abuse of dominance

  • Merger control filing
