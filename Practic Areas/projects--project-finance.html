Projects & Project Finance

Home Projects & Project Finance

Constantine Advisory Services Law lawyers have acted for lenders, borrowers, shareholders and international institutions on a range of projects across a number of sectors including electricity, coal, gas, renewable energy, roads, hospitals, rail, water, mines and minerals, ports, logistics and aviation.

Our Projects & Project Finance team advises on a range of matters including:


  • Bid documentation for project development and regulatory advice

  • Foreign direct investment and external commercial borrowing rules relating to the financing of projects

  • Bankability of project structures, allocation of risk and effective risk mitigation

  • Concession agreements, construction contracts, FIDIC contracts, operation & maintenance agreements, performance bonds, licenses and ancillary documentation

  • Finance and security documentation, joint venture and equity subscription arrangements, loan documentation, intercreditor arrangements and security and guarantee issues from term sheet to execution
